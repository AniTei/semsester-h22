Our exhibitions

The aim of our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries, and perhaps even ignite a passion to continue to explore the world around us. In addition to our regular exhibitions, we also host lectures, exhibitions on loan, and sometimes even a sleepover.
